Welcome to Gers Bikes

Gers Bikes is a Dublin bicycle shop located in Dublin 8 close to Irelands best scenic coastline bike route. We are a customer service-driven company which prides itself on our extended opening hours and precision mechanics. Stop in for a repair, a quick browse or even just a bicycle chat. Our friendly and passionate staff/cyclists will be more than happy to assist you. Advice is always free.

Bikes

We stock a wide range of bikes, including road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, and electric bikes.

Tunning and Bike Services

Gers Bikes is a bike repair shop and bike rental business that operates in Dublin Ireland. We also offer services such as bike maintenance classes, bike fitting, and the sale of new and used bicycles. Take advantage of our bike rent service in Ireland by either visiting us on-site or by booking an appointment so you can personally meet and speak with one of our cycling experts on-site.

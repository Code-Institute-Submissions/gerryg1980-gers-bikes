About Us

Gers Bikes is a family-owned bike shop located in the heart of the city. We have been serving the community for over 20 years and take pride in providing top-quality bikes and accessories to our customers.

Our team of experienced mechanics can help you with all your bike repair and maintenance needs. We also offer a wide range of bikes for all ages and skill levels, from kids' balance bikes to high-end racing bikes.
